How to install

1

Install BepInEx first

If you haven't already, download BepInEx 5.x and drop its contents into your game's root folder. This is required for any mod to work.

2

Grab the right download

Pick your game up top and download the .dll file. Make sure you get the one that matches the game you're playing.

3

Drop it in your plugins folder

Move the .dll into BepInEx/plugins/ inside your game folder. If the folder doesn't exist yet, just create it.

4

Launch the game and press Tab

Start your game and hit TAB to open the FCM menu. Click "Load Camera" and you're good to go!
